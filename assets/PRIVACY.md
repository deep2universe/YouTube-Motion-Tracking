## 2. How the Extension Works

This extension adds Halloween animation effects to YouTube videos. It uses AI-powered real-time pose detection to recognize body movements in videos and overlay animations (e.g., glowing skeletons, pumpkin heads, bats, ghosts) based on those movements.

**Important:** All video analysis and pose detection is performed entirely locally in your browser. No video data, images, or analysis results are transmitted to external servers.

---

## 3. What Data Is Processed?

### 3.1 Local Storage (chrome.storage)

The extension uses the Chrome Storage API to store your settings and preferences locally. This includes:

- Selected animation effects
- Activation/deactivation status of the extension
- Custom settings (e.g., animation intensity)

*This data remains exclusively on your device and is not transmitted to me or third parties.*

### 3.2 Local Video Analysis

For pose detection, video frames of the currently playing YouTube video are analyzed. This processing:

- Takes place entirely locally in your browser
- Does not transmit any data to external servers
- Does not permanently store any video frames or analysis results
- Only runs when the extension is active

### 3.3 No Collection of Personal Data

The extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. In particular, the following are **not** collected:

- IP addresses or device identifiers
- Your YouTube account or login credentials
- Your browsing history or search behavior
- The videos you watch
- Location data

---

## 4. Browser Permissions Used

The extension requires the following permissions to function:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `activeTab` | Allows access to the active tab to insert animations into the YouTube video. |
| `tabs` | Required to detect whether the current tab is a YouTube page. |
| `webNavigation` | Enables the extension to activate correctly during page transitions within YouTube (e.g., when switching between videos). |
| `storage` | Stores your settings locally on your device (no cloud synchronization). |
| `host_permissions` (youtube.com) | Restricts the extension exclusively to YouTube. The extension has no access to other websites. |

## 5. Data Sharing with Third Parties

**No data is shared with third parties.**
